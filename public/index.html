Love is Love

We met on good old Tinder! Our first date took us to the state fair, where we rode a Ferris wheel and had a great time. Right away, we sensed that something was different and that this relationship was special. About six months later, Cody was offered an internship in Arizona and asked if we both wanted to move there together for the summer. That summer was what really made us fall fully in love. At the end of that summer, we went on a cruise to Barcelona and fell in love with travel! We are so happy to have found each other and can’t wait to celebrate this day with everyone!
